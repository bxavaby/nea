Nautical Polar Night (naponi)


[WIP] naponi is an enjoyable palette that honors a restricted version of the polar night phenomenon.
Such phenomenon occurs at latitudes between (approximately) 72° 34' and 78° 34' N/S.

The Palette

  • #0c3c52
  • #d8bfd8
  • #fb9c9c
  • #8accd3
  • #434c5e

As aforementioned, this isn't a finished product.
I plan to scale it up to 8 colors soon and develop a comprehensive (16-color) version later.
